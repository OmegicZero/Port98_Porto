On Narrative Writing by Finn Gram Mertens

Ever dreamed of signing your life away to authorship?

Perhaps you've had an imagination beyond your grasp.

Look no further. Here are three principles every writer ought to know.

1. Show & tell, respecitvely when you must

How can I show you something without telling you about it?

2. All nighters & coffee are your best friends

The silent hours of night are best lived energetically

3. Don't seek to appease a single reader

You'll exit the world the same way you entered

4. Cut off any filler words, any fat

Your favorite anime series should take this principle to heart

5. Strive not to finish, but obsess to edit your piece

Written pieces never are perfect, though imperfections make pieces human
